The Kindly Koala

Fun Fact: The koala has one of the smallest brains in proportion to body weight of any mammal

  • Genus: Phascolarctos
  • Species: P. cinerus
  • Body Lenght: 60-85 cm
  • Weight: 4-15kg
While Koalas are often referred to as bears, they are actually marsupials. Koalas are herbivorous and like eating eucalypt leaves. Because they get so little energy for their diet, koalas must limit their energy use and sleep about 20 hours a day. They spent most of that time in trees. Koalas are asocial and spent only 15 min a day on social behavior.
